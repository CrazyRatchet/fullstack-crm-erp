# MVP Scope

## Includes

- User authentication and authorization
- Multi-tenant company management within each stack
- Contact and lead management (CRM)
- Product and service management
- Sales and basic invoicing
- Inventory control
- Basic reports and dashboards
  
## Not Included
  
- Banking or financial institution integrations
- Official electronic invoicing or tax compliance
- Advanced accounting features
- Fully featured native mobile applications
- Artificial intelligence or machine learning features

## Future versions

- Payment gateway integrations
- Advanced reporting and analytics
- Process automation and workflow management
- Industry-specific modules and customizations
